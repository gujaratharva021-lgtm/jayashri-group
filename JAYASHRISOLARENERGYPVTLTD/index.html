// Who We Are

BUILT ON CLEAN ENERGY,
DRIVEN BY PURPOSE

Incorporated under the Companies Act 2013, Jayashri Solar Energy Private Limited is a Maharashtra-based company committed to accelerating India's transition to renewable energy through solar power solutions.

Our mission is to generate, develop, and supply solar energy and renewable energy solutions — covering the full spectrum from installation and operation to manufacturing, trading, and consultancy. We partner with individuals, businesses, and institutions to deliver solar infrastructure that is reliable, efficient, and future-ready.

Registered as a Micro Enterprise under the MSME framework, we combine the agility of a focused team with the rigour of a formally incorporated private limited company.
