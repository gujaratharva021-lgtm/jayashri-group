Who We Are

A New Force in Construction & Manufacturing

Jayashri Constrotech Private Limited was incorporated on 19th August 2025 under the Companies Act, 2013. Registered in Maharashtra and headquartered in Rajgurunagar, Khed, Pune, we are a company limited by shares with strong capabilities across construction, metal fabrication, and concrete product manufacturing.

Our multi-sector approach allows us to serve clients with an integrated offering — from structural metalwork and doors/windows to full building construction on own-account or contract basis.
