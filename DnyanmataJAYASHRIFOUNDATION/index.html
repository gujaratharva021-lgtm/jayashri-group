Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Knowledge & Compassion

Dnyanmata Jayashri Foundation was incorporated on 6th February 2026 under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013 — as a Company Limited by Guarantee and Not Having Share Capital. Our scope extends to the Whole of India.

We are committed to operating on a non-profit basis. All income and surplus are applied solely towards the promotion of our charitable objects — education, women empowerment, healthcare, rural development, and inclusion of neurodiverse individuals.

The Foundation collaborates with Central and State Governments, local authorities, NGOs, trusts, and donors to achieve sustainable social impact.

Our Purpose

Mission & Focus Areas

We work across six core areas to create meaningful, lasting change for the communities we serve.

📚

Education & Skill Development

Promoting education, special education, vocational training, and skill development for underprivileged, orphan, and economically weaker students. Scholarships, coaching, and learning facilities.

👩

Women Empowerment

Education, vocational training, entrepreneurship development, financial literacy, self-help groups, and awareness programmes to improve the social, economic, and personal status of women and girls.

🏥

Healthcare & Wellness

Preventive healthcare, sanitation, nutrition, medical camps, maternal and child health initiatives, and facilitating access to basic medical services for underserved communities.

🌱

Rural & Community Development

Awareness programmes on education, health, hygiene, sanitation, and sustainable livelihoods — building stronger, self-reliant rural communities.

🧩

Neurodiversity & Inclusion

Research centres, therapy hubs, early diagnosis support, and inclusive education for neurodiverse children. Training for parents, educators, and caregivers.

🌿

Environmental Sustainability

Awareness on environmental protection, conservation of natural resources, promotion of cleanliness, sanitation, and sustainable practices for community well-being.

Our Approach

How We Create Impact

🏫

Establish Institutions

Schools, hostels, training centers, libraries, and digital learning platforms for orphan children, girl students, and marginalized communities.

🔬

Research & Resource Hubs

Analytical and scientific work on early diagnosis, therapeutic interventions, and inclusive education for neurodiverse populations.

🤝

Strategic Partnerships

Legal Structure

Section 8 Company — What It Means

The Foundation is incorporated as a Section 8 Company under the Companies Act, 2013 — the most transparent and accountable non-profit structure in India. This means:

All income and surplus are used only to promote the Foundation's charitable objects — no dividends to members.
Fully registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) with a valid CIN, PAN, and TAN.
Subject to statutory audit, annual filings, and full financial transparency under the Companies Act.
Eligible to receive grants, CSR funds, and donations from institutions, trusts, corporates, and individuals.
Can collaborate with Central and State Governments, NGOs, and international organisations.
No distribution of profits — any surplus on winding up goes to a similar Section 8 company or Rehabilitation Fund.
