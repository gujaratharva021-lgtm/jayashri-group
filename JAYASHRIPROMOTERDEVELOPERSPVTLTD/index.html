Who We Are

A Trusted Name in Real Estate Development

Jayashri Promoter & Developers Private Limited was incorporated on 3rd April 2024 under the Companies Act, 2013. Registered in Maharashtra and headquartered in Rajgurunagar, Pune, we are a company limited by shares with a focus on quality real estate development.

Our mission is to deliver well-planned, legally compliant, and customer-centric properties — from residential housing to commercial spaces — across the Pune region and beyond.
