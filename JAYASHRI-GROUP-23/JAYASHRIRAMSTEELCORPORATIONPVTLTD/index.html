// Who We Are

STRENGTH IN STEEL,
BUILT TO LAST

Incorporated under the Companies Act 2013, Jayashri Ram Steel Corporation Private Limited is a Maharashtra-based steel manufacturing company committed to producing high-quality iron and steel products for India's growing industrial and infrastructure sectors.

We carry on the business of manufacturing, processing, melting, refining, rolling, drawing, casting, converting, alloying, fabricating, producing, treating and finishing all kinds of iron and steel products — including billets, ingots, slabs, hot-rolled and cold-rolled steel, wire rods, steel wires, coils, sheets, plates, bars, rods, and structural steel.

Registered as a Micro Manufacturing Enterprise under MSME (NIC code 24109 — Manufacture of Other Basic Iron and Steel), we operate with a focus on quality, compliance, and industrial excellence.

// What We Make

IRON & STEEL
PRODUCTS & PROCESSES

From raw melting to finished structural steel, we cover the complete spectrum of iron and steel manufacturing processes and products.

🏗
Billets, Ingots & Slabs

Manufacturing of basic steel semi-finished products including billets, ingots, slabs, and blooms used as raw material for downstream rolling and fabrication.

🔩
Hot & Cold Rolled Products

Production of hot-rolled and cold-rolled steel sheets, coils, plates, and strips for construction, automotive, and general engineering applications.

🌀
Wire Rods & Steel Wires

Manufacturing of wire rods, cold-drawn wires, binding wire, fencing wire, barbed wire, industrial wire, and other ferrous wire products through cold drawing and stretching processes.

🏭
Structural Steel & Bars

Production of bars, rods, angles, channels, and structural steel sections for building construction, bridges, and heavy engineering projects.

⚗️
Melting, Refining & Alloying

Operations covering melting, refining, and alloying of steel — including operation of furnaces, rolling mills, and finishing facilities for diverse steel grades.

🔧
Surface Treatment & Finishing

Galvanizing, coating, annealing, cutting, slitting, and straightening services to deliver finished steel products meeting industry specifications.
