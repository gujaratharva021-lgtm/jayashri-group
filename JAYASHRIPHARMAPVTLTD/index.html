// Who We Are

QUALITY PHARMA,
BUILT ON TRUST

Incorporated under the Companies Act 2013, Jayashri Pharma Private Limited is a Maharashtra-based pharmaceutical company committed to manufacturing and distributing high-quality medicines and healthcare products.

We carry on the business of trading, buying, selling, importing, exporting, distributing, and dealing in all types of pharmaceutical products — including formulations, Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs), Over-the-Counter (OTC) drugs, prescription drugs, medical devices, and related healthcare consumables.

As a registered MSME Manufacturing Enterprise under NIC code 21002 (Manufacture of Allopathic Pharmaceutical Preparations), we serve both domestic and international markets in full compliance with applicable laws and licensing requirements.

// What We Offer

PHARMACEUTICAL
PRODUCTS & SERVICES

From raw APIs to finished formulations, we cover the full spectrum of pharmaceutical products for hospitals, pharmacies, distributors, and export markets.

💊
Pharmaceutical Formulations

Manufacture and distribution of allopathic pharmaceutical preparations including tablets, capsules, syrups, injections, and topical formulations.

🧪
Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs)

Sourcing, trading, and supply of high-purity APIs for pharmaceutical manufacturers, compliant with all pharmacopoeial and regulatory standards.

🏥
OTC & Prescription Drugs

Wide range of Over-the-Counter drugs and Schedule H/H1 prescription medicines for retail pharmacies, hospitals, and institutional buyers.

🩺
Medical Devices & Consumables

Supply of medical devices, diagnostic equipment, surgical consumables, and healthcare accessories for clinical and hospital use.

🌍
Import & Export

International pharmaceutical trading — importing raw materials and exporting finished products to global markets in compliance with DGFT and customs regulations.

🏪
Wholesale & Distribution

Acting as authorised wholesalers, stockists, agents, and distributors for domestic pharmaceutical brands, covering Maharashtra and pan-India markets.
