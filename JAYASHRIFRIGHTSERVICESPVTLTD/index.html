// Who We Are

TRUSTED CUSTOMS &
FREIGHT SPECIALISTS

Incorporated under the Companies Act 2013, Jayashri Freight Services Private Limited is a Maharashtra-based Customs Clearing and Forwarding Agency committed to seamless cross-border trade and logistics.

We act as agents for importers and exporters in the clearance and forwarding of goods through customs — handling documentation, compliance advisory, and last-mile coordination. Our services span foreign trade logistics, multi-modal transport, and courier clearances, helping businesses trade without borders.

As a registered MSME Micro Enterprise, we combine the speed of a focused team with the rigour of a fully incorporated private limited company under MCA's oversight.

// What We Do

END-TO-END FREIGHT &
CUSTOMS SOLUTIONS

From customs clearance to multi-modal logistics, we manage every link in your import-export chain.

🛃
Customs Clearance

End-to-end customs clearing for imports and exports — Bill of Entry filing, duty assessment, and liaison with customs authorities at all major Indian ports.

📦
Freight Forwarding

Sea freight, air freight, and door-to-door forwarding solutions for international shipments with complete tracking and documentation support.

📋
Customs Documentation

Preparation and processing of all customs documents — shipping bills, bills of lading, certificates of origin, and import-export declarations.

⚖️
Trade Compliance Advisory

Expert guidance on customs duties, import-export regulations, EXIM policy compliance, and duty optimisation strategies for your business.

🚛
Multi-Modal Transport

Integrated transport solutions combining road, rail, sea, and air — ensuring timely and cost-effective cargo movement across India and globally.

📬
Courier Clearances

Fast-track customs clearance for international courier shipments and express cargo, ensuring minimal dwell time and quick delivery.
