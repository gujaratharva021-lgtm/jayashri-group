Who We Are

Crafting Cinematic
Experiences

Jayashri Cinematics Private Limited is a Pune-based film and media production company incorporated on 20 August 2025 under the Companies Act, 2013. Registered as a Micro enterprise under MSME (Udyam No. UDYAM-MH-26-1121860), we are committed to producing world-class audio-visual content.

From blockbuster motion pictures to compelling web series, documentaries to television programs — we handle every stage of production with creative excellence and technical precision.

  • End-to-end film & web series production for theatrical and OTT release
  • Professional post-production: VFX, editing, sound, dubbing & subtitling
  • Event management, film festivals & talent coordination
  • IP acquisition — scripts, copyrights, trademarks, remake rights
  • Equipment import/export for filmmaking & digital content creation
