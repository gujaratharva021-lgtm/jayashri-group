Who We Are

Fashion Reimagined
for Every Season

Jayashri Fashion House Private Limited is a Pune-based fashion company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013. We operate as a Micro Enterprise registered under Udyam (MSME), committed to making quality fashion accessible across India.

From design to delivery — we handle manufacturing, sourcing, wholesale, and retail of apparel, garments, textiles, footwear, and lifestyle accessories.

👗

Fashion Design

Crafting unique styles rooted in Indian sensibility with modern aesthetics.

🏭

Manufacturing

In-house production of high-quality garments and apparel.

🌐

Import & Export

Global sourcing and export of fashion products and textiles.

🏷️

Brand Building

Developing proprietary and licensed fashion brands for the market.

What We Offer

Our Services

A complete fashion ecosystem — from raw material to retail shelf, we cover every step of the value chain.

01

Designing & Manufacturing

Full-cycle garment manufacturing from concept to finished product — apparel, garments, textiles and clothing accessories.

02

Wholesale & Retail

Supply to wholesale distributors and direct retail operations for fashion apparel, footwear and lifestyle products.

03

Import & Export

International sourcing of fabrics and accessories, and export of finished fashion products to global markets.

04

Fashion Consulting

Styling, visual merchandising, event planning and brand development services for individuals and businesses.

05

Brand Development

Building proprietary fashion brands and working with licensed third-party brands for market expansion.

06

Marketing & Promotions

End-to-end marketing, promotional services and event management for fashion launches and brand campaigns.
