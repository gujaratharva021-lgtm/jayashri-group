About Us

Who We Are

Charmakar Nivara Association is a Section 8 (not-for-profit) company incorporated on 30 May 2023. Originally registered with ROC Mumbai, the association shifted its registered office to ROC Pune in August 2025.

The word Charmakar means cobbler/leatherworker. Nivara means shelter. Our name is our promise.

  • Provide safe shelter and housing for underserved communities
  • Run educational institutions and training programs
  • Establish healthcare facilities and rehabilitation centres
  • Coordinate with government and NGOs for holistic development
What We Do

Our Four Pillars

Objects & Purpose
