About Us

A New Age Biofuel Company from Pune

Salasarbalaji Ethanol Private Limited is incorporated under the Companies Act 2013, dedicated to producing ethanol and agro-based biofuel products for India's growing clean energy sector.

⚡

Ethanol for Petrol Blending

Supplying biofuels directly to petroleum marketing companies to support the national E20 blending program.

🌾

Agro-Based Raw Materials

Processing sugarcane, molasses, and high-starch agro products into quality ethanol and allied chemicals.

🏭

Full Manufacturing Chain

From production and refining to storage and distribution — an end-to-end ethanol manufacturing enterprise.

What We Do

Products & Business Activities

Our MOA covers a wide range of biofuel, chemical, and agro-based manufacturing activities.

🔥

Ethanol Production

Manufacturing ethanol from sugarcane juice, molasses and starch-based feedstocks for blending in petrol as per government mandates.

Core Business
🧪

Rectified Spirit & Alcohol

All grades and formulations of alcohol products including rectified spirit for commercial and industrial applications.

Manufacturing
🌱

Agro Products

Trading and processing of sugar, sugarcane, and high-starch agro-based products as raw materials and by-products.

Agro-based
⚗️

Industrial Chemicals

Organic and inorganic chemicals derived from fermentation including specialty gases, bio-fertilizers and effluent gases.

Chemicals

Petroleum Products

Trading and supply of petroleum, lubricating oils, hydrocarbons, petrochemicals including Ethylene and Propylene.
