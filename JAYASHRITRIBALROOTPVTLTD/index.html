🌿About the Company

Bridging Tribal Wisdom
with Modern Markets

Incorporated on 17 July 2025, Jayashri Tribalroot Pvt. Ltd. is a manufacturing enterprise committed to sourcing, processing, and marketing authentic tribal and organic food products from tribal communities of Maharashtra.

Our Story

We were born from a deep commitment to preserving the rich culinary heritage of tribal communities. By establishing food processing units, cold storage facilities, and distribution networks, we create a direct bridge between tribal producers and urban consumers.

Our approach ensures fair trade, sustainable sourcing, and the preservation of traditional knowledge — while bringing the nutritional richness of forest-based and organic produce to tables across India and beyond.

We invest in R&D and innovation to enhance the quality and shelf-life of traditional tribal foods without compromising their cultural and nutritional integrity.

🎯Our Purpose

What Drives Us

Every product we make carries a commitment — to the communities that grow it, the land it comes from, and the people who consume it.

🤝

Community Empowerment

Directly partnering with tribal farmers and communities — ensuring fair compensation, capacity building, and economic upliftment through every transaction.

🌍

Sustainable Sourcing

Procuring raw materials responsibly from tribal lands using sustainable agricultural practices that protect forest ecosystems and biodiversity.

📚

Heritage Preservation

Documenting, promoting, and sustaining the traditional culinary knowledge and agricultural wisdom of tribal communities for future generations.

🏭

Modern Infrastructure

Building food processing units, cold storage facilities, and distribution networks that give tribal produce the reach and longevity it deserves.

🔬

Innovation in Food Tech

Investing in research and food technology to improve product quality, shelf-life, and marketability without compromising nutritional integrity.

🌐

Market Access

Connecting tribal producers to domestic and international markets — giving their products a platform they deserve and consumers access to the finest organic foods.
